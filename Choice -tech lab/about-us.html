ABOUT US

What’s In The Name?

As a subsidiary of the Choice Group (est. 1993), we retain our lineage in our name with the intent to stay grounded in the value-added system of established expertise. We do this while evolving with the changing times and algorithms, which is why Technology is at the crux of everything we do. And since all great innovations happen in a Lab; ours is a place where creativity is allowed to run wild; monitored only within the rationality of science.

Making Magic with Experiments.

Our Vision

Our Mission

Our Essence

Our journey

How IT Started

We started off as an E-Commerce Portal and faced a fair share of struggles when it came to an IT team. A well-qualified team was close to impossible to find and issues arose in the form of disagreements in delivery or being charged unreasonably. So we built an in-house IT team from scratch and shifted our focus to being a full-fledged IT Company; from seven we have grown to a hundred

Headquartered in Mumbai, we’ve become an organisation of technologists, creatives and strategists; offering consolidated services right from design and development to marketing and security. It could be anything from marketing in an online world, to creating web and mobile apps that make the world go round, or cloud infrastructure consulting.
